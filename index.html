Case Studies

Aromatica360: Building Brand Awareness Through Storytelling

A comprehensive social media strategy for a home fragrance brand

The Challenge

Aromatica360, a new entrant in the competitive home fragrance market, needed to establish brand awareness and create a distinctive voice that would resonate with their target audience of home decor enthusiasts and wellness-focused consumers.

The Approach

I developed a content strategy centered around sensory storytelling, creating immersive content that highlighted how fragrances can transform spaces and enhance wellbeing. The strategy included:

  • Daily themed content across Instagram and Facebook
  • Weekly "Scent Stories" featuring customer experiences
  • Educational content about fragrance notes and benefits
  • Lifestyle photography showcasing products in beautiful home settings

28%

The Result

Within three months, Aromatica360 established a strong brand presence with a highly engaged community. The content strategy not only increased brand awareness but directly contributed to a 32% increase in online sales. The "Scent Stories" series became a customer favorite, generating user-generated content and building brand loyalty.

Claro: Establishing Authority in Real Estate Evaluation

Educational content strategy for Egypt's first private real estate evaluation company

The Challenge

As Egypt's first private real estate evaluation company, Claro needed to educate the market about their services while establishing themselves as the authoritative voice in a new industry category.

The Approach

I created a multi-platform educational content strategy focused on demystifying real estate evaluation and highlighting its importance for investors, homeowners, and developers. The strategy included:

  • Weekly blog articles explaining evaluation methodologies
  • "Ask Claro" YouTube series answering common questions
  • Infographics breaking down complex evaluation concepts
  • Case studies showcasing successful evaluations

The Result

The content strategy successfully positioned Claro as the go-to authority for real estate evaluation in Egypt. The "Ask Claro" series became particularly successful, with several videos exceeding 50,000 views. The educational approach not only built brand awareness but created a more informed customer base that understood the value of professional evaluation services.
